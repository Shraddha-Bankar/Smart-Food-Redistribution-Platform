# Abstract

Food waste and food insecurity are two major global challenges that coexist despite advances in food production and supply chain management. The Smart Food Redistribution Platform is a technology-driven solution designed to connect food donors, NGOs, volunteers, and beneficiaries to reduce food waste and support communities in need. The platform enables restaurants, hotels, supermarkets, event organizers, and individuals to donate surplus food through a centralized digital system.

The platform uses real-time donation management, intelligent matching, and route optimization to ensure that surplus food reaches the right recipients before it expires. Donors can list available food items, NGOs can request donations, and volunteers can coordinate deliveries through an interactive dashboard. Live tracking and notification features improve transparency and operational efficiency.

The primary objective of the project is to minimize food waste, promote sustainable resource utilization, and contribute to hunger reduction. By integrating modern web technologies and smart logistics, the Smart Food Redistribution Platform demonstrates how digital innovation can address real-world social and environmental challenges effectively.

# Introduction

Food waste is a growing global problem, with large quantities of edible food discarded every day while many people suffer from hunger and malnutrition. Restaurants, hotels, supermarkets, and event organizers often have surplus food that could be redistributed instead of being wasted.

The Smart Food Redistribution Platform provides a digital solution to bridge the gap between food donors and organizations that serve people in need. The system facilitates efficient food donation, management, and delivery while reducing waste and improving accessibility.

### Objectives

- Reduce food wastage.
- Connect food donors with NGOs and volunteers.
- Enable efficient food distribution.
- Improve delivery management.
- Promote sustainable and responsible food consumption.

# Methodology

The Smart Food Redistribution Platform operates through a centralized web application where donors register surplus food along with details such as quantity, location, and expiry time. NGOs browse available donations and submit requests based on their needs. The system prioritizes food distribution according to urgency and location while optimizing delivery routes for volunteers. Real-time notifications and tracking improve coordination between all stakeholders. The platform maintains donation records and provides analytics to monitor food waste reduction and distribution efficiency, ensuring timely delivery and maximum social impact.
